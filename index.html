About Coastal Escape Tours

At Coastal Escape Tours, we are passionate about providing our customers with unforgettable experiences on our beautiful beaches. Our Mission is to offer personalized and luxurious tours that showcase the natural beauty, local culture, and hidden gems of the coast.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by a group of traverlers, Coastal Escape Tours began as a small operation focused on sharing the beauty of our favorite beach destinations. Over the years, we have expanded our offerings, but our commitment to personalized, high-quality tours has remained the same

From humble beginnings to becoming a leading provider of coastal tours, our journey has been fueled by our love for the ocean and our desire to create memorable expereinces for our customers
